### Data Deletion Instructions
Users can request deletion of their personal data by following these steps:
1. Open the app and navigate to **Settings > Privacy > Delete Account**.
2. Confirm the deletion request by entering the registered email address or Facebook login credentials.
3. All personal data, including profile information, activity logs, and stored preferences, will be permanently removed 
   from our servers within 30 days.
4. Users can also contact our support team at **support@myapp.com** to request immediate data deletion.
